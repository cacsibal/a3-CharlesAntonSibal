Todo List Web App

Description: This is a simple web app designed to help manage tasks. It allows users to add, edit, and delete tasks.

Instructions: To sign up, enter username and password and the account will be made automatically upon pressing the login button. If you already have an account, please enter your username and password to log in. In your dashboard, you can add, edit, delete, and mark tasks as completed. You can also create categories to organize your tasks.
